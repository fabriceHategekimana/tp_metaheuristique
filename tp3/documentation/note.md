### Initial_temperature

We will try to isolate the $T_0$:

$$
e^{\frac{-(|\Delta E|)}{T_0}} = 0.5
$$

$$
ln(e^{\frac{-(|\Delta E|)}{T_0}}) = ln(0.5)
$$

$$
\frac{-(|\Delta E|)}{T_0} = ln(0.5)
$$

$$
T_0 = \frac{-(|\Delta E|)}{ln(0.5)}
$$
